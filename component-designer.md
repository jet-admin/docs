Component Designer has five distinct regions:&#x20;

1. **Toolbar** ‚Äì find tools to create frames, shapes, text, and other layers. These will be the building blocks of your designs.
2. **Parameters** ‚Äì allow you to pass dynamic variables from your app to the component
3. **Left sidebar** ‚Äì gives you access to layers. Explore any layers on the canvas.
4. **Canvas** ‚Äì is your working area where you‚Äôll arrange layers to create your designs. You‚Äôll find lots of room to explore and iterate on your ideas.
5. **Right sidebar** ‚Äì access more information about your designs. With a layer selected, you can explore the properties of your current selection.

### Conditional Visibility&#x20;

**Conditional Visibility** allows dynamic display adjustments of UI components or layers based on specified conditions. This feature is handy when you need UI elements to respond to changes, such as altering the color of a metric indicator depending on whether its value is positive or negative.
